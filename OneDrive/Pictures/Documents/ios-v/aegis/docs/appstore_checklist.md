App Store & entitlement submission checklist

- Prepare human-readable justification for Network Extension usage and privacy practices.
- Provide Apple with a demo account and test steps showing how the app uses the entitlement.
- Include screenshots and a video of the app exercising content-filter / URL filter behavior.
- Document telemetry collection, retention, and opt-in flow in the App Privacy section.
- For enterprise distribution, include MDM/supervision notes and deployment instructions.
